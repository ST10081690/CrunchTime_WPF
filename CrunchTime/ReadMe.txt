--------------WELCOME TO CRUNCH TIME--------------
--------------------------------------------------
Crunch Time has the goal of helping students plan 
out a study schedule, so they can keep their focus
and avoid stress.
--------------------------------------------------
INSTRUCTIONS--------------------------------------
When you run the app, you will be prompted to
either login or register if you are new.

Please press "Register" if it is your first time
using the application. If not then you can press
"Login" and proceed.
--

[Register]
When this window opens, you are prompted to enter
your details, such as username, first name, etc.
Under "field you are studying", just enter the name
of the field ,for example: Civil Engineering.

(!)Please take note of the username and password 
that you enter, as these will be required for you 
to log into the app after registering.
--

[Login]
This window will prompt you to enter the username
and password that you used to register into the app.

(!)Please make sure you enter the exact details you
used upon registration. Any other input will not be
approved.
--

[Main Menu]
After being validated as a user you will be directed
to the main menu window. You can choose the function
you want to access through this menu. But first, make
sure you press "Calculate Study Hours" to add 
your semester and module details.
(!)You won't be able to access any other function
without any module details saved.
--

[Calculate Study Hours - Semester Details]
In this window, you will be required to enter
information regarding your academic semester. 
(!)Please only enter numbers in this window, make
sure you enter a start date, and leave no empty
boxes.
--

[Calculate Study Hours - Module Details]
In this window, you are required to enter details
about each module you are taking. So you will be 
prompted to enter the module code, name, and other
details for each module. After entering details 
for a module, you'll move on to the next one by 
pressing "Next".
(!)All boxes are required in this window, make
sure you only enter number for credits and class
hours.
--

[View Study Hours]
You will be brought to this window automatically
once you have finished saving modules through the
"Calculate Study Hours" function. But you can also
directly access this window through the Main Menu,
if you have modules saved.

Once you entered information for all your modules,
the application will calculate the self study hours
for each of the modules you are taking, and it will
display the module name and the self study hours
per week.
You can use this information to plan out your week
and schedule your "Crunch Time" and self study 
hours.
--

[Schedule/Record Study Hours]
When you choose this function through the main menu,
you have the option to record/schedule study hours
for a module on a date. You will be prompted to select
a module, out of the modules you saved, and to enter
details about the study day. 

You can press "Next" to save the current details and
enter information for another module, if you wish
to do so. Press "Done" once you no longer have study 
days to schedule or record.

(!)Please enter a number for study hours. Make sure
you select a date and make sure you select a module.
Only press "Next" if you need to enter hours for
another module, if that's not the case, press "Done".
--

[View Remaining Hours]
After entering the self study hours that you have
planned or completed, the application will calculate
and display the remaining self study hours for that
module. So you can keep track of your progress. You
will be brought to this window automatically after
scheduling/recording study hours, but you can access
this window directly, from the main menu, if you have
scheduled/recorded study hours.
(!)You can only access this window, after 
scheduling/recording study hours for at least one (1)
module that you saved.
--
To exit the application, press the "Exit" button.
--
